By harnessing the potential of microbes, we engage in business areas that develop, produce and distribute high value raw materials for pharmaceuticals, health food and cosmetics.

We produce pharmaceutical-grade raw materials, including phycocyanin and chlorophyll, renowned for their antioxidant properties, anti-cancer attributes, immunoregulatory effects, and antimicrobial qualities. These substances serve as photosensitizers, making them suitable for application in Photodynamic Therapy (PDT), a cancer treatment solution.

Our cosmetic grade raw materials include Nannochloropsis salina and Porphyridium cruentum , which is capable of skin-healing effects and producing colour pigments, exemplify our commitment to diverse and sustainable applications.

Last but certainly not least, our food-grade raw materials encompass the increasingly popular Long Linear Spirulina Platensis (NCB002) and Chlorella vulgaris, both boasting substantial amounts of chlorophyll A.

Ncellbio has the technical prowess to produce a diverse range of industrially valuable compounds from micro-organisms. These compounds include chlorophyll, phycocyanin, omega-3 fatty acids and omega-6 fatty acids. Our environmentally and human-friendly extraction processes emphasise our dedication to sustainable practices.
