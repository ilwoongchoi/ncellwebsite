About the company

Ncellbio is a biotech company specializing in cultivation of micro organisms, such as bacteria, fungi and microalgae. Of all the microorganisms, our primary emphasis is on microalgae. Our flagship strain is Long Linear Spirulina Platensis(NCB002), a distinctive variant of the Arthrospira platensis species. Additionally, our diverse strain portfolio also includes Chlorella vulgaris, Micractinium reisseri, Nannochloropsis salina, Porphyridium cruentum, schizochytrium etc.

Our research and development efforts are dedicated to creating functional materials and foods, such as immune active substances, antioxidants, and antibacterial agents through natural substances obtained from natural microorganisms.

Our Staff

Structured in a collaborative team-based fashion, our company comprises 4 divisions: department of management, department of overseas business, department of cultivation and department of R&D. Each department plays a crucial role in developing and manufacturing products that enhance the health and well-being of our customers.
